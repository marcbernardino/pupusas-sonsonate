Pupusas Sonsonate

The Chef

Elsa immigrated to the United States when she was only 14 years old during the civil war that was happening in her home country of El Salvador. She did this not only for safety, but also to have a better life and achieve what is known as the American Dream. After working at a fast food establishment for over 15 years, she decided it was time for a change. She no longer wanted to work for someone, but rather, she wanted to be her own boss. Elsa started saving her money to ultimately open her restaraunt business, Pupusas Sonsonate. She has slowly been building her clientele and honing her cooking skills during this time by catering for small events. She is skilled with cooking dishes from El Salvador, Ecuador, and Mexico.

The Restaurant

Pupusas Sonsonate was created to bring authentic flavors and dishes from El Salvador and other countries to Arizona. Each dish is cooked with such passion and flavor your taste buds will thank you! Our mission here at Pupusas Sonsonate is to make sure every customer here leaves happy, full, and intrigued by what other delicacies El Salvador has to offer!
